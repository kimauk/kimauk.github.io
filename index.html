RESEARCH INTEREST

I am a researcher in Interactive Computing LAB (Advisor: Uichin Lee) at KAIST, South Korea. While my research interests span across various areas of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Ubiquitous Computing, currently, I am focusing in "Understanding and Designing Human Interactions with Artificial Intelligence Systems". I obtained my Ph.D. in the Department of Knowledge Service Engineering at KAIST in Feb, 2020.
